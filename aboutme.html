My aim is to detect human pain points/needs through empathy and curiosity and solve them with creativity. With the knowledge gained, I develop ideas and concepts that are tested and validated using prototypes. On that way, I am always seeking to learn something new and collaborate with people from all different skill sets to create interdisciplinary and complex solutions. The combination of design and code offers me to think through and understand the overall creation process of a new product. My focus lays on user-centric UX (and UI) design with design thinking methods and prototyping/code as my main tools.

My desire to be a designer profounds in my dream to contribute something impactful to the world. And I really love to tinker, prototyping is quite close to that. Furthermore I am passioned about start-ups und hackathons. Check out my CV for my skillset and my previous work experiences! :)

In March 2021, I successfully completed my studies in "Internet of Things - Design of Networked Systems" at the HfG Schwäbisch Gmünd. There I learned methods and tools to design user-centered concepts and solutions.
